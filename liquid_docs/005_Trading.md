Trading
Liquid offers many fun products to trade, like gold and crypto. This section explains the full extent of trading on Liquid.
Spot Trading
Liquid offers spot trading through the search bar. 
On Liquid, you can purchase assets like Bitcoin, Ethereum, Solana, and the HYPE token, for no fees! In the United States, Liquid is by far the lowest-fee solution for purchasing Bitcoin. 
Trading with Multipliers
Liquid's signature feature is letting you trade with multipliers. If you want to buy $100,000 of Bitcoin, you need $100,000 (and potentially thousands more to pay fees). However, on Liquid, you can buy exposure to $100,000 of Bitcoin with a mere $2,500 and a 40x multiplier. You can risk less to earn more!
